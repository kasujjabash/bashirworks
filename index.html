Preparing for our MoonShot

The Elevate Booth

The Elevate Booth is a prototype of the Elevate Academy that is created as a strategic approach to scale the Elevate Academy across Uganda & Africa. This is a Digital Classroom in a Shipping Container. It’s an instrument that will accelerate targets for reach and impact of the organisation’s two-year strategic plan and vision 2025 from today. The key areas of the strategy and vision 2025 that the booth is addressing are reaching 10,000 youths and creating 5,000 jobs by the year 2025. You want to be a part of our moonshot?
